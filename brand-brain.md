> Living document. Each agent reads this before acting. Director updates monthly.

## What KIND2 is
DTC haircare. Built for women dealing with hair shedding/thinning at life-stage triggers (postpartum, menopause). Founder-led. Hero product line: scalp + hair growth system.

## Audience (ICP)
- Women 28-55, UK first.
- Triggers: postpartum hair shed, perimenopause/menopause, post-Covid telogen effluvium.
- Mindset: results-led, not interested in fluff. Read reviews carefully. Trust real testimonials.
- Channels they're on: Instagram, TikTok, YouTube (long-form reviews), Mumsnet-adjacent communities.

## Voice
Warm, knowledgeable, peer-to-peer. Speaks results, not "wellness". Cites the science but doesn't lecture. UK English.

Banned: hype, white-room beauty-counter speak, gendered patronising ("ladies", "babes"), miracle claims.

## Differentiators
- Real before/after photos with timeframes.
- Founder story credibility.
- Scientific backing without lab-coat tone.

## Risks the agents should watch

- Reviews-based marketing must be VOC, not invented.
- Refund cycle is 60-day; CS allow-list takes that into account.
